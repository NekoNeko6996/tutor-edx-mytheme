1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the platform, enroll in a course, or contact us for support. This may include your name, email address, profile photo, phone number, and any other details you share in your profile.

Additionally, we automatically collect some technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and usage data to help us improve system performance and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your learning account.
  • To deliver courses, assessments, and certificates.
  • To personalize your learning experience and recommend relevant content.
  • To communicate important updates, newsletters, or promotional offers (you can opt out anytime).
  • To maintain security and prevent unauthorized access.

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement appropriate administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, modification, disclosure, or destruction.

All sensitive data, such as passwords, are encrypted using industry-standard technologies. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, but some parts of the site may not function properly without them.

5. Third-Party Services

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or services, such as payment gateways or analytics tools. Please note that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you choose to close your account, we will delete your personal information within a reasonable timeframe, except when retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. You may also request that we limit or stop processing your data. To exercise these rights, please contact our support team at support@cusc.vn.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Updates will be posted on this page with the effective date clearly indicated.
